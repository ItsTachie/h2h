Safety Guidelines

At HandToHand, your safety is our priority. Please follow these tips when buying or selling on our platform.

1. Meet in Safe, Public Places

Always arrange to meet in well-lit, busy public areas like shopping centers, service stations, or police stations. Avoid secluded spots or meeting at night.

2. Bring a Friend or Family Member

If possible, take someone you trust with you when meeting a buyer or seller. Two sets of eyes are always better than one.

3. Verify Before You Pay

Inspect the item carefully before handing over money. For electronics, test them on the spot. Don't send money before seeing the product.

4. Communicate Through WhatsApp

We encourage using WhatsApp so you can keep a record of your conversations. Avoid moving to other messaging apps where scammers may try to hide their tracks.

5. Trust Your Instincts

If something feels off, walk away. It's better to miss out on a deal than to put yourself at risk.

6. Report Suspicious Listings

If you come across a suspicious listing or user, please report it immediately through our platform so we can take action.
