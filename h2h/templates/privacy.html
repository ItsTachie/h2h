Privacy Policy

Effective Date: August 2025

At HandToHand, your privacy matters to us. This policy explains how we handle your data when you use our marketplace.

Information We Collect

  • Basic account details: name, email, WhatsApp number
  • Listing information: photos, descriptions, prices, and locations you submit
  • Technical data: browser type, IP address, and usage logs

How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage your account
  • To show and promote your listings
  • To contact you regarding account or listing issues
  • To improve and secure our services

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We only share limited details (like your WhatsApp number) with other users when you interact with their listings.

Your Rights

You can request deletion of your account and data by contacting us at support@handtohand.site.

You can also delete your account and data in your account dashboard

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page.

If you have questions, contact us at support@handtohand.site.
